JOURNEYING

Journeying? This is a blog site or in other words written podcasts of techies. This is intended to be inspirational upon reading some of uploaded blogs of techies who have made it in the field. May be you are embarking on the technology journey or you have been in this space for a while now, you will get one or two stories that will inspire you to be where you aspire to be in the tech space. You can also be the one who uploads their stories and with that you can motivate others who would want or are already in the tech space. I hope you will find this space useful on your journey in tech or will give you enough encouragement to join this tech space if you were considering joining us. In short this is meant to inspire, encourage and help the techies in the building. Be the first to Inspire and to be Inspired on your journey to tech! Welcome to this Space!!.
